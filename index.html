Research

My research advances real-time image and video analysis through high-speed algorithms for feature tracking using deep learning. I enhance spatial tracking accuracy with techniques like PIPs and RAFT, and develop a Transformer-based method for precise keypoint tracking, improving applications such as Structure from Motion. In applied research, I use deep learning to predict mechanical properties of carbon nanotube (CNT) forests and analyze their growth and shape. I also create precise video analysis pipelines for detecting horse lameness through skeletal movement and develop a cross-platform 3D measurement tool for large photogrammetric datasets.
